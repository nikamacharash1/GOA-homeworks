New York City

Prices

  • Flights from Los Angeles: $250-$500
  • Flights from Chicago: $200-$400
  • Flights from Miami: $300-$600
  • Hotel prices: $150-$300 per night
  • Hostel prices: $30-$60 per night
  • Food prices: $15-$30 per meal
  • Transportation prices: $3-$6 per ride
  • Attraction prices: $20-$50 per person

Top Attractions

  • Times Square
  • Central Park
  • Statue of Liberty
  • Empire State Building
  • Metropolitan Museum of Art

Travel Tips

  • The best time to visit New York City is in the spring or fall.
  • Be prepared for crowds, especially during peak season.
  • The city is very walkable, but be sure to wear comfortable shoes.
  • Public transportation is very efficient, but can get crowded during rush hour.

Things to Know

  • The cost of living in New York City is very high.
  • The city is very diverse, with people from all over the world.
  • New York City is a 24/7 city, with something to do at any time of day or night.

  • 4-night hotel stay and flight from Chicago: $900-$1,400
  • 5-night hotel stay and flight from Miami: $1,200-$1,800
